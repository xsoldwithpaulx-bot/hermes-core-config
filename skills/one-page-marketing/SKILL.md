# one-page-marketing

category: marketing
trust: local

## description

Implements a 1-page marketing plan specifically for a Southern California real estate agent focused on seller leads (La Mirada, LA, Orange County). Uses the “Before/During/After” 9-block grid to design a complete seller funnel around the agent’s core listing offer.

## instructions

When this skill is active, you:

- Assume you are building a marketing plan for the same agent and offer defined by the `hundred-million-offers` skill.
- Always structure outputs using the 1-page marketing plan grid:

  BEFORE (Lead generation)
  - Target market
  - Message
  - Media

  DURING (Conversion)
  - Lead capture
  - Lead nurture
  - Sales conversion

  AFTER (Lifetime value)
  - World-class experience
  - Increase customer lifetime value
  - Referrals and advocacy

- Tailor everything to seller leads in:
  - La Mirada
  - Surrounding LA / Orange County suburbs
- For each block, provide:
  - 1–3 concrete tactics.
  - Examples and, when relevant, exact copy or scripts.

- Priorities for this agent:
  - Consistent seller lead flow that feeds the “Grand Slam” listing offer.
  - Channels: direct mail, door knocking, online lead forms, social, sphere, past clients.
  - Assets: scorecard/quiz lead magnet, CMA-style reports, short video scripts, email sequences.

- Do NOT output vague advice. Always give specific examples, scripts, subject lines, or templates the agent can deploy immediately.
- Do NOT mention SKILL.md or the source book. Just present the plan.

## examples

User: “Build a 1-page marketing plan to generate and convert seller leads in La Mirada using my strong listing offer.”

Assistant:
- Fills in all 9 blocks with specific tactics, copy, and sequences tailored to La Mirada homeowners.
- Shows how the offer is used in the Message, Lead Capture, and Sales Conversion blocks.
